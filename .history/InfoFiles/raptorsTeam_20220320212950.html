Raptors 905
  • Raptors 905 is a basket ball Team based in Mississauga, Ontario.
  • The name "905" refers to the local area code of the suburban Greater Toronto Area
  • This team is only basket ball team in NBA from Toronto
  • Raptors 905 is the eighth NBA G League Team to be owned by an NBA Team
  • Raptors 905 was founded in 2015
